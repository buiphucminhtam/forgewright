## Direction 1: Shopee Affiliate via Midscene

### How It Works
1. Midscene opens real Safari/Chrome on Mac
2. Searches Shopee by trending keywords (serum, kem chống nắng, etc.)
3. AI vision parses product cards from screenshots
4. Saves real product URLs to products.json
5. Midscene opens affiliate.shopee.vn, generates custom links
6. Facebook poster with 3 MiniMax images + affiliate link
7. Track clicks/conversions via Shopee dashboard

### Revenue Model
- **Commission rate:** 1-5% depending on category
- **Average product price:** 100,000-500,000 VND
- **Commission per sale:** 1,000-25,000 VND
- **CPS (cost per sale):** Only pays when someone buys
- **Traffic needed:** ~500 clicks/month for ~5 sales = ~250,000 VND/month

### Time to First Revenue
- **Week 1:** Midscene research + conversion pipeline working
- **Week 2:** First 3-5 posts with real affiliate links
- **Week 3-4:** First potential conversions (if traffic exists)
- **Realistic first sale:** 2-4 weeks IF content reaches audience

### Technical Complexity: 7/10
- Midscene script already created
- Shopee UI may change → maintenance required
- Need to parse images with AI vision
- MiniMax image gen = $0.05-0.10 per post

### Risk Assessment
| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|------------|
| Shopee blocks Mac IP | Low | High | Use VPN if needed |
| Midscene fails on Shopee UI | Medium | High | Manual fallback for critical steps |
| No conversions despite traffic | High | Medium | Test different products/angles |
| Affiliate link expiries | Medium | Low | Refresh links monthly |

### Cost Analysis
- **MiniMax API:** ~$10/month (already paying)
- **Midscene:** Free (uses existing Mac)
- **Shopee account:** Free
- **Facebook posting:** Free
- **Total additional cost:** $0
- **Revenue needed to break even:** $10/month = ~500,000 VND commission/month

### 30/60/90 Day Roadmap
| Phase | Days | Goal |
|-------|------|------|
| Week 1-2 | 0-14 | Midscene research + convert working. First 10 posts with affiliate links |
| Week 3-4 | 14-28 | First conversions. Identify top-converting product categories |
| Month 2 | 30-60 | 50 posts/month. 10-20 conversions. ~500,000 VND revenue |

### SWOT Analysis

|----------|----------|

| **Opportunity** | **Threat** |
